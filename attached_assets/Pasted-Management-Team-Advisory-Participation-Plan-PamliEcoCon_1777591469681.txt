Compensation & Incentive Philosophy
PamliEcoConnect’s compensation philosophy is built around:

Capital efficiency in the R&D phase – minimizing fixed cash outlay in favor of funding vessel development, USCG approval, and operational pilots.
Upside alignment – offering meaningful equity‑based incentives to those who take on ongoing management responsibilities.
Performance and commitment – reserving the most substantial upside for individuals who move beyond advisory roles into sustained operational leadership.
Accordingly:

Advisory roles may receive modest cash stipends and/or small equity grants (subject to board approval), reflecting their strategic input and introductions.
Individuals who later transition from advisory positions into formal management roles will be eligible for management‑level equity participation, as described below.
Future Management Equity Participation Structure
As the company secures initial financing and moves into full vessel development and operations, it intends to implement a Management & Key Contributor Equity Pool representing approximately 10–20% of the fully diluted equity, subject to board and legal approval.

Key elements of the anticipated plan:

Founders (CEO and CFO) hold meaningful common equity positions reflecting their early risk and contribution.
Advisors who transition into active management roles (Design Lead, M/E Engineering Lead, Battery/Powerplant Lead, Compliance Lead, Production Lead, etc.) may receive significant equity grants sized according to their role, time commitment, and impact.
Equity will be delivered through standard instruments (e.g., stock options or restricted stock/units) with:
4‑year vesting and a 1‑year cliff
Ongoing vesting monthly or quarterly thereafter
Performance‑based “kicker” grants tied to major milestones such as:
USCG approval and entry into service of the first 20‑passenger R&D vessels
Completion and approval of the 50‑passenger vessel design
Execution of significant commercial, institutional, or defense contracts
Double‑trigger acceleration upon change of control plus termination or material reduction in role for active managers
This framework allows PamliEcoConnect to:

Engage high‑caliber experts early as advisors, with low cash cost and high strategic value.
Offer a clear, attractive pathway into formal management roles for those advisors who choose to commit more deeply.
Align long‑term upside with the individuals who help carry the company from prototype and regulatory approval through to scaled operations and potential strategic exit or public offering.
